The Most Exciting Round at Wimbledon

Often, the most crowd-pleasing points of tennis are the long rallies, where the players challenge each other by moving around the court, with one ultimately putting away the point with a rousing winner. By the stats, these points would translate to a low number of aces and errors, with a high average distance travelled per point by all players. This chart graphs these metrics across each round of play at Wimbledon 2016 in the men's tournament.
